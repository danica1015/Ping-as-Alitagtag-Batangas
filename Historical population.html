Historical population



The population of Ping-As grew from 761 in 1990 to 1,595 in 2020, an increase of 834 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 2.15%, or an increase of 153 people, from the previous population of 1,442 in 2015
